Fretboard Learning

Fretboard Learning is a true guitar companion. You can practice and learn the notes of your guitar in your favorite tuning, learn any scale of any key and any chord over the fretboard at any time.

This app is developed and maintained by a guitar player just like you. So, all suggestions you might have to improve it are welcome.

How it works?

You'll be starting with few notes and frets to play with in the begining.
To unlock the rest of the notes and frets you have to play any of the game modes available to gain points.
