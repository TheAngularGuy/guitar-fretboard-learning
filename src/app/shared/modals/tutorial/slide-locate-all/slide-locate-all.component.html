Game Modes:

Locate all

In this game mode, you'll have to find all occurrences of a note between in an area of the fretboard (that is unlocked).

Once you unlock the first twelve frets, you can chose the use all of them as the playing area.

The same goes for the 22 frets of the fretboard. Once they are all unlocked, you can choose the option to play with all the frets as a playing area.
In this cas you'll have to find all occurrences of a note in the entire fretboard.
